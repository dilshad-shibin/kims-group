History About Kims

Kims Group established in 1988 is an authorised pharmaceutical distributor and wholesaler representing many top-tier manufacturing companies.The company especially deals with distribution of pharmaceutical and surgical items in Kerala by adopting global distribution standards. The companies have been operating under a valid drug license issued by the Government of India under the Drugs & Cosmetics Act 1940.

Today, the company has been providing the best Service in terms of Quality, Cost and Timelines, hence making rapid inroads into the much-competitive global pharmaceutical industry.The company and its pharmaceutical branches are situated in Kottakal, Malappuram district.

Besides the pharmaceutical bussiness,the company has extended to the areas of gold and petroleum business ,ie Kims Gold and Kims Petroleum.The professionals at Kims have always striven to maintain highest quality standards.The company is succesfully running its journey with no compromise in customer satisfaction.
