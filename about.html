History About KIMS

KIMS Pharmaceuticals is an accredited medical and pharmaceuticals wholesale distributors based in Malappuram in Kerala, India with services covering most of Thrissur To Kasargod districts.

We are a licensed wholesale distributor of prescription / branded and generic medicines as well as dental healthcare medicines etc.
